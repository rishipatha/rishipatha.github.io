Science

Primary Sanskrit Texts Related to Science

  • Sulbasūtras (Geometry)
  • Āryabhaṭīya (Astronomy & Mathematics)
  • Bṛhat Saṃhitā (Varāhamihira)
  • Vaisheshika Sūtra (Philosophy of atomism)
  • Siddhānta Śiromani (Bhāskara II)

Ancient Indian science spans mathematics, astronomy, metallurgy, and more. Texts such as the Sulbasūtras delve into geometry for constructing Vedic altars, while Āryabhaṭa and Bhāskara II wrote extensively on planetary motion and advanced numerical techniques.
