Philosophy

Primary Sanskrit Texts of Indian Philosophy

  • Upaniṣads (e.g., Bṛhadāraṇyaka, Chāndogya)
  • Brahma Sūtras
  • Bhagavad Gītā
  • Nyāya Sūtras
  • Sāṃkhya Kārikā

Indian philosophy is generally divided into six classical schools: Nyāya, Vaiśeṣika, Sāṃkhya, Yoga, Pūrva Mīmāṃsā, and Vedānta. Each system explores reality, consciousness, ethics, and liberation in its own way.
