Literature

This section explores timeless Sanskrit texts that have enriched Indian culture and philosophy.

  • Ramayana – The epic story of Prince Rama, his exile, and his battle for righteousness.
    "रामो राजमणिः सत्यवान् पराक्रमः।"
  • Mahabharata – The vast epic recounting the struggle for sovereignty culminating in the battle of Kurukshetra.
    "धर्मक्षेत्रे कुरुक्षेत्रे समवेता युयुत्सवः।"
  • Bhagavad Gita – A philosophical dialogue on duty and righteousness, part of the Mahabharata.
    "कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।"
