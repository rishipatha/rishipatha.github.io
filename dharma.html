Dharma

Key Sanskrit Texts on Dharma

  • Manusmṛti
  • Yājñavalkya Smṛti
  • Mahābhārata (especially Śānti Parva)
  • Ramāyaṇa (discussion of righteous conduct)

Dharma signifies the moral, ethical, and righteous path. It encompasses duty, virtue, and cosmic law. Through Dharma, individuals align with universal harmony, ensuring social stability and personal spiritual growth.
