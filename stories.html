Stories

Primary Sanskrit Story Collections

  • Pañcatantra
  • Hitopadeśa
  • Purāṇas (e.g., Bhāgavata Purāṇa)
  • Itihāsas (Rāmāyaṇa and Mahābhārata)

Storytelling holds a treasured place in Indian culture. Epics like the Rāmāyaṇa and the Mahābhārata teach moral and spiritual lessons, while Pañcatantra and Hitopadeśa present fables about wisdom and practical life lessons.
