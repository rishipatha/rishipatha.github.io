Ayurveda

Primary Sanskrit Texts of Ayurveda

  • Charaka Saṃhitā
  • Sushruta Saṃhitā
  • Aṣṭāṅga Hṛdaya (by Vāgbhaṭa)
  • Bhela Saṃhitā
  • Śārṅgadhara Saṃhitā

Ayurveda is the traditional system of medicine from the Indian subcontinent. It emphasizes balance in bodily systems using diet, herbal treatment, and yogic breathing. The key pillars involve balancing three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
